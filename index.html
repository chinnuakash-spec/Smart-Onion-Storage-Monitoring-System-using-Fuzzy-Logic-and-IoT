Smart Onion Storage Monitoring

About Project

This project highlights the key features and objectives of our system — a testament to the symbiosis of hardware and software in addressing contemporary challenges in agriculture. By leveraging modern technology, we aspire to enhance post-harvest practices, ensuring a sustainable and efficient supply chain for the fundamental agricultural commodity — onions.

The sensors act as sensory organs, collecting real-time data on temperature, humidity, and gas concentrations. The Raspberry Pi 3B+ serves as the brain, orchestrating data collection, processing, and transmission — integrating a Django backend with a robust SQLite database.
